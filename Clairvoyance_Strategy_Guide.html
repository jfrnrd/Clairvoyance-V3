Why Combinatorial Markets Matter

Real world complexity: Events don't happen in isolation‚Äîthey influence each other. Betting on combinations lets you express nuanced views about how the world works.

Better information aggregation: Markets reveal not just what might happen, but how different events relate. Valuable for decision makers, researchers, and businesses.

Market efficiency: Express your complete thesis in one position instead of fragmented liquidity across multiple simple markets.

Higher engagement: Building complex positions requires thought and analysis. Larger potential payoffs for taking on more risk.
